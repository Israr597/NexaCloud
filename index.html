Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NexaCloud

Is my data really secure with NexaCloud?

Yes! NexaCloud uses AES-256 encryption, which is the same standard used by governments and financial institutions to protect sensitive data. Your files are encrypted both during transfer and while at rest in our servers.

Can I access my files offline?

Yes, with our desktop and mobile apps you can mark files for offline access. These files will be available even when you don't have an internet connection, and will sync automatically when you reconnect.

How does the free trial work?

Our 30-day free trial gives you full access to all Professional plan features. No credit card is required to start the trial. At the end of 30 days, you can choose to continue with a paid plan or downgrade to our free Basic plan.

What happens if I exceed my storage limit?

If you exceed your storage limit, you'll receive a notification and won't be able to upload new files until you either free up space or upgrade your plan. Your existing files will remain accessible.
